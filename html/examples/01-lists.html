Scrambled Eggs

Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over medium heat.
  2. Gently mix the eggs and the cream in a bowl.
  3. Once butter has melted, add cream and eggs.
  4. Using a spatula, fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 seconds.
  5. When the eggs still moist, remove from the heat. (It will continue to cook on the plate until served.)
